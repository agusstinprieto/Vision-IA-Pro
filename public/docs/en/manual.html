Instructions for Drivers and Inspectors

1

Check-In: Start Route

Scan the **QR Code** fixed on the truck's chassis to link your trip. The secure camera system will activate.

Important Note

Ensure good lighting. Blurry photos trigger preventive yellow alerts.

2

The "360 Walkaround" (Tires)

Capture each tire following the application order. Maintain a 1-meter distance from the tread.

Correct: Perpendicular
Incorrect: Angled
3

Cargo Zone (Seals and Gas)

Capture the **Rotogauge** ensuring the needle is visible. Then, take photos of the security seals on the main valves.

What to do in case of a RED ALERT?

If a critical discrepancy is detected by AI at the end of the trip, the system will block trip closure.

  1. Do not move the unit from the unloading area.
  2. Immediately contact your Operations Supervisor.
  3. Wait for manual inspection and the Manager's override code.
